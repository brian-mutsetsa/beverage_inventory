AI POWERED MOBILE SUPPLY CHAIN OPTIMIZATION 

Introduction
The Supply chain encompasses everything from the delivery of inventory from suppliers to the 
delivery of goods to the end users. The supply chain is a complete process from order signing to 
product arrival in the hands of customers(Lin, Lin and Wang, 2022). Small businesses often 
manage these processes manually, using pen and paper. Since small businesses have limited 
resources, they cannot afford better supply chain management systems  like Systems Application 
and Products (SAP) or Oracle. Manual methods are prone to error, inefficiency, and delays, 
which result in poor visibility across operations. The main issue faced by the small businesses is 
resulting problems where there will be frequent stockouts, overstocking, delayed deliveries and 
unhappy customers. The integration of supply chain activities and the technologies like artificial 
intelligence  have become competitive necessities in most industries(Amini et al.2023). Large 
companies are well recognized the benefits of SCM, but small and medium enterprises (SMEs) 
are lagging behind in appreciating how integrated supply chain drives remarkable changes in 
business processes and work with positive results in better quality services, cost reduction and 
efficiency( Chin,Hamid,Rasli and Baharun 2020).
To tackle these issues, the, we propose the development of a AI-powered supply chain 
optimization system. Since the system is for small businesses to be exact, it will be a mobile
enabled application. The decision to make it a mobile-enabled application came from the fact 
that almost everyone has access to a cellphone and they are less expensive than having to buy 
laptops or desktop for every employee. The application will feature inventory management that 
will help monitor or track stock levels, order management  for recording customer and supplier 
orders and cloud syncing for backup and storage of the data. Cloud sync enables users to 
maintain the same copy of data in real time, which eases users the tedious yet error-prone data 
management burden(Chen,Li,Jiang,Xiang and Yang 2022). The mobile application will make 
integrate artificial intelligence for forecast demand. Most current mobile applications work with 
a back-end server over the Internet. But in many occasions network connectivity is not available 
and a proper offline treatment must be provided(Fernandes et al 2022). Lastly, the application 
will have an offline support system to ensure some functions are not interrupted even without 
internet access.
Problem Statement
Small businesses encounter more challenges in handling their supply chain processes, such as 
inventory, procurement and order fulfilment. The challenges are due to overreliance on manual 
methods such as spreadsheets and paper and pen which  result in inefficiency, delays and 
frequent stockouts or overstocking. Another challenge these businesses are facing is lack of 
offline support. The reason for using manual methods is that small businesses have a limited 
budget unlike large organization that can invest in supply chain management (SCM) software. In 
the long run, these challenges will eventually weaken the relationship between the business and 
both the customers as well as the suppliers. The absence of affordable real time systems leaves 
the small businesses vulnerable and unable to compete effectively. 
To tackle these issues, we propose AI-powered mobile enabled supply chain optimization 
application with integrated offline support to ensure uninterrupted functionality, cloud sync for 
real-time data access, artificial intelligence/ machine learning driven demand forecasting will 
provide a solution for the small businesses. It will also be able to perform action like inventory 
tracking and order management. This system will generate operations even if the mobile device 
is offline and instant data access when back online. The proposed system will cover the gap 
between tight budgets and the need for innovative supply chain management.
Aim
To enhance the efficiency and reliability of supply chain processes in small businesses by 
providing offline support, cloud synchronization, and AI/ML-powered demand forecasting.
Objectives
1) To track stock levels
2) To automatically update inventory
3) To monitor  customer orders
4) To synchronize data with the cloud for storage



Methodology
This project will make use of the mixed research methodology as well as the kanban agile 
methodology to ensure that the supply chain optimization system addresses the real issues faced 
by small businesses. Mixed research methodology will be conducted to collect data through one
on-one interviews with the small business owners and questionnaires to the employees to gain 
more knowledge on the current supply chain management and expectations. The research will 
proceed by collecting data from books and online articles that discuss supply chain optimization 
and cloud-based systems. The obtained data will be analyzed to identify recurring problems in a 
day-to-day operation. As the research phase comes to an end, kanban agile methodology will be 
used to start the development of the mobile application. Since  Kanban supports incremental 
delivery, it will give room for the small business owners and employees to test each functionality 
and give feedback. Limits on work in progress (WIP) will keep the development cycle focused 
and productive. For the testing stage, unit testing will be included for verifying individual 
functions as well as integration testing to confirm whether the cloud syncing and offline support 
will work together with the system seamlessly. The system will be deployed on mobile devices 
to test it out in the real market and collect feedback.
Project Scope
The supply chain  management system will be developed as a mobile application that is 
compatible with Android devices. It will utilize a local database for storing transactions offline. 
It will  include AI/ Machine learning for demand forecasting. Machine learning is widely used in 
managing different areas and aspects of the supply chain(Tirkolaee et al., 2021). The project 
scope will not include blockchain technology because it requires significant investment in 
training and maintenance. It also adds unnecessary complexity.
Feasibility Study
The proposed project is feasible in all aspects, be it technical, operational or economic. When 
looking at the technical feasibility aspect, the supply chain management system  relies on the 
available and well-supported technologies. From the economic aspect,  the system will go 
through the whole development process at a minimal cost. Since mobile devices are widely 
owned, there won’t be any need for costly additional infrastructure, making everything 
affordable. As for the operational aspect, by simplifying order and inventory management 
through a mobile user-friendly interface, the system will be accessible to users with limited 
technical knowledge. 
Solution Application Area
The project Supply Chain optimization system will be mainly used in the small sized businesses.  
The proposed project is of some real value because it will help improve the small businesses’ 
competitiveness due to the addition of the offline support which makes the system easy to use in 
areas with unstable internet connectivity. This will be beneficial to the small businesses owners 
because it will help reduce losses from stockouts.
